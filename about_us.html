Pat Wilkins was born and raised in Forks, WA., where salmon fishing and farming is just a natural part of life. A graduate of the University of Washington, Pat opened shop in 2005 after deciding that coding just wasn't for him. 13 years later, Salmon-Cookies are a hit. We invite you to share in the love and enjoy the Wilkin's family recipe.

Pat lives with his wife, Sheryl, in Mercer Island. Sheryl is an investment banker with an international portfolio of ultra-high-net-worth individuals. They have 2 Children, Sam and Will, who won't be able to find jobs as adults.
